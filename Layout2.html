About Us

BKNS Government Polytechnic, Gopalganj is a leading diploma engineering institute under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of Bihar. The institute offers modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-friendly learning environment. With a strong focus on technical excellence, we prepare students to meet industry demands and build successful careers in engineering fields.

Course Offered

We offer 3-year diploma courses in the following engineering branches:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
Admission is based on the DCECE (Diploma Entrance Exam) conducted by the Bihar Government. �
